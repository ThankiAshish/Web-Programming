Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering Department was established in the year 2009. The department offer B.Tech program in Civil Engineering with a yearly intake of 120 students. A group of qualified faculty and adequate experience is the highlight of the department. The department has qualified faculty members occupied with educating and research having perfection in different fields. The faculty members are youthful, dynamic and prepared to meet the scholastic needs yearning of understudies.

The objective of the B.Tech graduate program in Civil Engineering is to give the understudies great comprehension of civil engineering arrangements in a global, societal and ecological setting, steady with the standards of sustainable improvement. The course will make the understudies capable Identifying, planning and taking care of civil engineering problems that meet indicated execution, cost, time, well being and other quality needs and objectives with proficient and moral responsibility. The department also offers two PG programs, M. Tech. in Structural Engineering and M.Tech. in Construction Technology under full time category.

➤ Vision of the department

  • To impart knowledge and excellence in Civil Engineering and technology with global perspectives to our students and to make them ethically strong engineers to build our nation.
  • To produce Civil Engineers of high caliber, technical skills and ethical values to serve the society and nation.
  • To make the department a centre of excellence in the field of civil engineering and allied research.
  • To provide knowledge base and consultancy services to the community in all areas of civil engineering.
  • To promote innovative and original thinking in the of budding engineers to face the challenges of the future.

➤ Program specific outcomes

  1. Graduates Shall have an ability to analyze and solve problems related to civil engineering using mathematics, Science and fundamental principles of engineering.
  2. Graduate shall have an ability to analyze, design, construct, maintain and rehabilitate the infrastructural projects, using the knowledge of subjects related to planning, construction, structural, geotechnical, transportation, environment and water resource engineering as well as project management.
  3. Graduates shall have the ability to employ advanced civil equipments, softwares, and techniques.
  4. Graduate shall have an ability to apply gained knowledge to choose from the innovative career paths, to be an entrepreneur, and a zest for higher studies.
  5. Graduates shall have an ability to interact and work seamlessly in teams.
