Computer Engineering

Department of Computer Engineering is established in year 2006. The department offers BTech and MTech programs in computer engineering.The department also offers PhD program in Computer Engineering. Since its inception, BTech(CE) program was offered with intake of 60. Present intake is 120.

It is a four years (eight semester) full-time undergraduate program. Out of eight semesters, seven semesters are dedicated to in house study involving course work and projects. Final semester is dedicated to Industrial Project. Students spend entire eighth semester in an IT company and do a project.The curriculum is designed by keeping industrial and research needs, as well as the rapid development in the IT industry. Flexibility in curriculum is provided through Electives, Seminars and Projects. The program is supported by well-equipped laboratories consisting of the state-of-the-art servers & computers. This creates a lively and stimulating atmosphere to study Computer Engineering. The Faculty members publish their research work in national and international journals and conferences.

➤ Vision of the department

  • The Computer Engineering department aims to maintain a program to excellence in teaching,research, and service for all of its program
  • This course will provide students with a firm foundation of both the theory and practice of Computer Engineering
  • Our curricula also give students the social, ethical, and liberal education needed to make significant contributions to society.
  • Our faculty are engaged in both practical and theoretical research, often in partnership with government agencies, private industry, and non-governmental organizations.
  • The aim of this research is to advance knowledge within our disciplines and also to contribute to solving problems faced by our society.

➤ Program specific outcomes

  1. Understand, analyze and develop computer programs in the areas related to algorithms, system software, multimedia, web design, big data analytics, and networking for efficient design of computer-based systems of varying complexity.
  2. Apply standard practices and strategies in software project development using open-ended programming environments to deliver a quality product for business success.
  3. Utilize multi-disciplinary knowledge required for satisfying industry requirements and hence develop an attitude for lifelong learning.
  4. Work professionally in software industries and develop the modern tools in IT fields.
